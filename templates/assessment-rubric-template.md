## Assessment Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent | Good | Needs Improvement | Score |
|---|---|---|---|---:|
| Project Structure | Folder structure is complete, clear, and well organized. | Folder structure is mostly complete and understandable. | Folder structure is incomplete or confusing. | /20 |
| Learning Outcomes | Learning outcomes are clear, measurable, and connected to the project. | Learning outcomes are mostly clear but need small improvements. | Learning outcomes are missing, unclear, or not measurable. | /15 |
| Learning Materials | Materials are clear, practical, and suitable for the target group. | Materials are understandable but need improvement. | Materials are incomplete or not suitable for the target group. | /20 |
| BMAD Method Application | BMAD Method is clearly visible in planning, content, assessment, and delivery. | BMAD Method is partly visible. | BMAD Method is missing or unclear. | /15 |
| AI Use and Improvement | AI support is used responsibly and the content is improved manually. | AI support is used, but improvement is limited. | AI use is unclear or copied without improvement. | /10 |
| Online/Offline Delivery | Project can be used clearly online and offline. | Project mostly supports online/offline use. | Online/offline delivery is unclear or missing. | /10 |
| Assessment Design | Tasks, evidence, and criteria are clear and transparent. | Assessment is included but needs improvement. | Assessment is missing or unclear. | /5 |
| Reflection | Reflection clearly explains learning, AI use, and improvement. | Reflection is present but general. | Reflection is missing or too short. | /5 |
